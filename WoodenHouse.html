Aitta & Sauna

The Built around 1790, the guesthouse - "aitta" - was originally a stable for horses and a storehouse. Now it is a tranquil space with a unique atmosphere of the times gone by. Hidden behind old doors you will find a beautiful bedroom with a comfortable king size bed, an elegant sauna, a modern shower and toilet , and a space for relaxing after sauna.
