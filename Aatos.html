Aatos café

After a trip around Porvoo, stopping for a delicious treat is a really good idea. And Aatos café is a ideally option. Aatos Cafe is a cozy place in Porvoo, which is located in the WSOY building. This is an easy approachable and attractive place for anyone who appreciates pure food and comprehensive comfort. They offer handmade breakfast and lunch, freshly squeezed juices and smoothies, different types of coffee as well as a high quality tea selection, organic wines and small breweries.
